About Fayoum University

Fayoum University is a leading public institution in Egypt dedicated to high-quality education and research. Located in the vibrant city of Fayoum, the university offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including Science, Engineering, Arts, Law and Medicine.
The modern campus features advanced facilities, including laboratories, libraries, and student accommodations. Fayoum University emphasizes research and community engagement, fostering a collaborative environment for students and faculty.
With a commitment to academic excellence and local impact, the university provides a dynamic campus life enriched by extracurricular activities and community service opportunities.
